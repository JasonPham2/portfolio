The Challenge

Users weren't inspired by insurance quotes; they were intimidated. Long forms, dense terminology, and product-first flows eroded trust before value appeared.

People don't wake up thinking "I need insurance." They think about buying a home, starting a family, or retiring comfortably. But traditional financial planning forced them to speak a foreign language first.

üìö

Too much jargon

Portals led with policy questions before users even understood how coverage supported their lives. The language felt cold and transactional.

üéØ

No personalization

Dense terminology and long forms chipped away at confidence before value was visible. Everyone got the same experience, regardless of their goals.

‚ùÑÔ∏è

Cold handoffs

Advisors received little context about users' dreams and priorities, so outreach felt generic instead of tailored and empathetic.

"Users don't plan by product. They plan by life event."

üéØ What if we flipped the script?

What if financial planning started with your dreams, not your documents?

The Shift

We reframed the intake question to unlock trust: "What life milestone matters most right now?"

By starting with goals instead of products, we created a human-first experience that feels less like paperwork and more like planning a meaningful future.

üåÑ

Start with goals

Users start by picking the milestones they care about, whether that's buying a home, starting a family, or planning for retirement. Their priorities lead the way, not ours.

üß≠

Guide with clarity

Plain language, visual timelines, and helpful nudges make progress tangible without overwhelming detail. Every step feels achievable.

ü§ù

Empower advisors

Goal context travels with every lead, so follow-up calls begin informed and empathetic. Advisors become partners, not salespeople.

üí° How we brought it to life

Five focused screens that translate dreams into actionable next steps

Step 01 ¬∑ Landing

A warm welcome, not a sales pitch

The mountain path hints at steady growth and the journey ahead. A single "Get Started" button invites people into the planner without pushing products. The moment feels calm, aspirational, and human.

Step 02 ¬∑ Goal Selection

Pick what matters to you

Users choose up to three milestones that matter most, whether that's buying a home, planning retirement, starting a family, or traveling the world. The interface adapts instantly to mirror their priorities. Their goals, their language.

Step 03 ¬∑ Timeline Builder

Map your future

A visual, interactive timeline transforms abstract ambitions into tangible plans. Drag-and-drop milestone chips map to age ranges, making the future feel real and within reach. Complex planning made simple.

Step 04 ¬∑ Your Personalized Plan

From goals to actionable next steps

Each milestone expands into practical next steps, educational resources, and relevant Co-operators offerings. Users can save, download, or email a shareable roadmap. One tap books an advisor with all that goal context already attached. It's the sweet spot where digital planning meets human guidance.

We built a bridge between aspiration and action. Digital tools meet human guidance here.
