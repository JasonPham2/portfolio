Overview

Resonate is built for beginners who want to grow at their own pace. Inspired by micro-learning apps like Duolingo, it condenses vocal coaching into rewarding loops that reduce anxiety and celebrate consistent effort.

01

Approachable practice loops

Three-minute warmups, guided exercises, and reflections keep routines digestible while nurturing daily momentum.

02

Adaptive guidance

Exercises calibrate to vocal range, tension, and goals, so feedback lands in the moment it is needed most.

03

Confidence-first tone

Inclusive copy, supportive visuals, and gentle rewards remove the fear of judgment and keep practice playful.

Guiding question: How might we help beginner singers build confidence by rewarding consistency and offering low-pressure support?

The problem

Interviews and surveys surfaced emotional barriers that technical tools rarely address. Singers needed motivation, feedback, and encouragement that felt human.

üìÖ

Practice slumps

Streaks fizzled after day two

Long, unstructured exercises were hard to squeeze in. Without gentle reminders, singers skipped practice altogether.

üéØ

Feedback drought

"Am I making progress?"

YouTube and prerecorded lessons couldn't confirm technique, so singers feared reinforcing mistakes in isolation.

üéµ

Technique overload

Too many cues, not enough clarity

Coach notes and PDFs arrived hours later, making it difficult to recall breathing, posture, and pitch guidance during practice.

üîí

Confidence blockers

Fear of judgment stalled sharing

Recording or practicing near others felt vulnerable, so singers avoided feedback and missed growth opportunities.

The solution

The final prototype combines habit-forming loops, adaptive coaching, and celebratory storytelling so singers feel guided and encouraged at every step.

Quick practice loop

Resonate breaks practice into short, rewarding sessions with a warmup, guided exercise, and reflection so singers never feel like they‚Äôre falling behind.

  • Adaptive pacing responds to vocal strain and energy so every loop feels doable.
  • Micro-rewards celebrate streaks and milestones without hijacking motivation.

Actionable goals

Weekly goal templates align to each singer‚Äôs confidence level and time, turning vague intentions into focused steps that keep momentum.

  • Goals adapt to range, routine, and feedback so progress always feels relevant.
  • Soft celebrations reinforce streaks without adding pressure or guilt.
  • Weekly reflections reconnect practice to personal motivations.

Progress storytelling

Progress storytelling turns streak history, pitch accuracy, and mood reflections into a narrative that validates effort and restores confidence.

  • Gradient rings show streaks, highlight restorative breaks, and avoid shame tactics.
  • Mood check-ins track emotional wins instead of just numbers.
  • Optional share-outs let singers loop in a coach or keep insights personal.
